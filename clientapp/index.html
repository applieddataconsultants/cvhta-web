Our Mission:
To foster growth, innovation and awareness of the Chippewa Valley's high-tech community


The Chippewa Valley High-Tech Alliance (CVHTA) is a group of area technology-based businesses that have come together to raise awareness of the region as a leader in technology. A large number of companies – past, present and emerging – either employ technology as the core of their business, or rely on technology to deliver their products or services.

We met for the first time in December, 2014 to discuss how we could work together to form a coalition to market the area as a career destination for those seeking careers in technology, as well as to show off the skills of the talent who are already employed in the area.

We decided to form our own coalition to showcase all the opportunities that are available in this region, as well as to display the innovative work performed by our talented employees.
